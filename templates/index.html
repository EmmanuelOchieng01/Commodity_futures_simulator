The Monte Carlo engine simulates 10,000 future price paths using Geometric Brownian Motion and calculates your revenue distribution under each hedging strategy.
No Hedge
High Risk
Full exposure to price movements. Revenue moves entirely with the market — maximum upside and maximum downside.
Full Hedge
Low Risk
Lock in the current futures price for 100% of volume. Revenue is fixed regardless of where prices move at expiry.
Partial Hedge
Medium Risk
Hedge 50% at the futures price, leave 50% exposed. Balances downside protection with upside participation.
Dynamic Hedge
Medium Risk
Adaptive coverage — increases protection when prices fall and reduces it when prices rise to capture the upside.
